Mae C. Jemison

Mae Jemison is an American chemical engineer and astronaut who became the first ever African American woman in space. She attended Stanford Engineering for her undergraduate and Weill Cornell Graduate School of Medicine for her doctorate. After boarding Endeavour in 1992, she now works as an educator and author, writing the memoir Find Where the Wind Goes in 2001 which detailed her experience as a woman of color in STEM education.
